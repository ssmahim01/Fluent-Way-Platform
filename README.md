# üéì FluentWay - English Language Training System

**FluentWay** is an advanced English language training platform designed to help learners enhance their fluency and proficiency in English. Whether users are preparing for IELTS, improving their spoken English, or mastering business communication, FluentWay offers a structured and interactive learning experience.

[![Website](https://img.shields.io/badge/Live%20Demo-Fluent%20Way%20-brightgreen)](https://fluent-way.vercel.app)

---

## üöÄ **Key Features**  

- **Diverse Course Offerings** ‚Äì Provides a range of English learning courses, including IELTS Preparation, Spoken English, Business English, Grammar, Writing Skills, and more.  
- **User-Friendly Interface** ‚Äì A seamless and intuitive platform built with Next.js for optimal performance.  
- **Course Management System** ‚Äì Users can explore, enroll, and manage their course bookings with ease.  
- **Progress Tracking** ‚Äì Allows students to update their learning status and track their progress.  
- **Secure Authentication** ‚Äì Integrates Next Auth for user registration and login.  
- **Responsive Design** ‚Äì Ensures accessibility across different devices, enhancing the learning experience.
